My Learning Plan

My Long Term Goal

To master the skills that are most valued and rewarded in the web development industry.

Non-technical or human skills I would like to develop while at Dev Academy

  • Communication: To develop my ability to communicate my values and ideas confidently, and with kindness and firmness. I want to be confident that I communicate in a way that it is easy to be received accurately, and to improve understanding.
  • Work ethic: I would also like to become someone I would 100% describe as hardworking and who has a strong work ethic.
  • Leadership: To lead myself, to work with clarity, purpose, awareness, and engagement.
  • Productivity: I want to develop the abilities necessary to produce effectively, efficiently, with grace and that reflects my best efforts.

What I expect from the facilitation team

I expect to feel safe with the facilitation team. To ask for help and support and know that I will be met with kindness, compassion and understanding. I also expect facilitators to be engaged and keep us accountable, and to be able to help when needed.

My commitments for managing the workload in this programme

If I start to get overwhelmed, I will:

  • practice self-empathy
  • try to formulate a plan focusing on the essentials
  • go for a walk, or a nature hike
  • go for a shower, sauna, float, or massage
  • talk to a trusted person, e.g. my boyfriend, a facilitator

I will aim to relate with others empathically, respectfully and in ways that grow trust. I will seek to meet commitments, deadlines, standards, and others' needs by making these explicit through writing (even if just in private) and planning: breaking down the tasks, scheduling these tasks (as well as everything in life), anticipating obstacles and listing ways to overcome these obstacles. I know how I show up is highly determined by my wellbeing...

I am committed to establish healthy habits to increase my capacity to do my best in this programme:

    For sleep:
  • Be in bed between 8pm-9pm every night.
  • To turn off devices and block blue light by 7pm.
  • To have a hot shower by 6:30pm.
  • To finish dinner by 6:00pm.

  • Exercise at least 30 minutes a day or get 10,000 steps.
  • Don't consume sugar, flour or vegetable oils.
  • Practice nose-belly breathing to a 6-1-8 in-pause-out count for 3 minutes.
  • Meditate for at least 11 minutes a day.
  • Practice gratitude.
  • Do Carpe Diem journaling from optimize.me

    • I will start with committing to practicing the sleep section starting 26 April 2022.
