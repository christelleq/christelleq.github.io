My Learning Plan

My Long Term Goal

To master the skills that are most valued and rewarded in the web development industry.

Non-technical or human skills I would like to develop while at Dev Academy

  • Communication: To develop my ability to communicate my values and ideas confidently, and with kindness and firmness. I want to be confident that I communicate in a way that it is easy to be received accurately, and to improve understanding.
  • Work ethic: I would also like to become someone I would 100% describe as hardworking and who has a strong work ethic.
  • Leadership: To lead myself, to work with clarity, purpose, awareness, and engagement.
  • Productivity: I want to develop the abilities necessary to produce effectively, efficiently, with grace and that reflects my best efforts.

What I expect from the facilitation team

I expect to feel safe with the facilitation team. To ask for help and support and know that I will be met with kindness, compassion and understanding. I also expect facilitators to be engaged and keep us accountable, and to be able to help when needed.
